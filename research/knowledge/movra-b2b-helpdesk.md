# Movra B2B Clinic Dashboard — Helpdesk Documentation
Source: https://haider-3.gitbook.io/product-docs

## Platform Overview
Movra operates as a health AI platform designed specifically for clinic operations. The Clinic Dashboard enables clinics to:
- Establish and configure clinic accounts with subscription activation
- Integrate payment processing through Stripe merchant accounts
- Design custom pricing structures for members through subscription plans
- Send member invitations with self-service payment options
- Monitor member engagement metrics and generate performance reports

Target audience: Clinic leadership, operational staff, and support personnel.

---

## Getting Started — 8-Step Setup (10–20 minutes)

### Prerequisites
- Access to the email you plan to use for Movra
- Stripe account ready for payment processing

### Setup Steps
1. **Account Creation** — Register at signup page
2. **Email Verification** — Input 4-digit code sent to email
3. **Subscription Activation** — Select a plan and complete payment
4. **Dashboard Access** — Log in with credentials
5. **Stripe Integration** — Complete Stripe onboarding to accept member payments
6. **Pricing Configuration** — Establish member subscription tiers (billing cycles, tier levels)
7. **Member Onboarding** — Send invitations or distribute payment link
8. **Functionality Testing** — Verify invitations and payment links work

### Recommended Next Steps
- Invite members to your clinic
- Configure subscription pricing structures
- Review analytics dashboards

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Account Access
- **Can't sign in?** Confirm correct email, complete verification, activate subscription
- **Forgot password?** Use "Forgot password" link on sign-in page

### Stripe & Payments
- **Can't create plans or invite members?** Complete Stripe onboarding, create at least one subscription plan, refresh
- **Stripe onboarding pending?** Return to Stripe to complete missing steps (identity, banking, business info)

### Member Onboarding
- **Invite link expired?** Resend invitation or share payment link
- **Member can't subscribe?** Verify Stripe connected, plan exists, member using latest link

### Reports
- **Dashboard/reports empty?** No members yet, not enough data, or loading issue (refresh)

### Additional Support
Contact support with a screenshot of the problem for unresolved issues.
